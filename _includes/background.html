Background

I work as a freelance product designer for Enclave and have previously interned at Snap Inc. and at Facebook. My current interests lie in how augmented and virtual reality is redefining human-computer interaction, storytelling, and the way we consume and create content.

Outside of debugging my code you can find me reading, watching movies or playing my synth.
